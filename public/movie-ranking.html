The Best Movies According to SRINIVAS

My Top 3 Movies of All The Time


Bhadra

"Bhadra" is a 2005 Telugu action movie. The story revolves around Bhadra, who falls in love with his friend Raja's sister, Anu. When Anu is threatened by Raja's enemies, Bhadra takes it upon himself to protect her. As the story unfolds, Bhadra's loyalty and love for Anu are tested. He faces numerous challenges and fights against all odds to keep Anu safe.The movie is a thrilling mix of action, romance, and drama, showcasing Bhadra's courage and determination. It ends with Bhadra defeating Raja's enemies and securing a safe and happy life for himself and Anu.

  • Release Date: 12 May 2005
  • Cast: Ravi Teja & Meera Jasmine
  • Director: Boyapati Srinu

    • Raja Rani

    "Raja Rani" is a touching tale of love, loss, and second chances. The story revolves around John and Regina, two individuals who are forced into an arranged marriage by their families. Both are dealing with the pain of past heartbreaks. John was deeply in love with Keerthana, who tragically died in an accident. Regina, on the other hand, was in love with Surya, who abandoned her.After their marriage, John and Regina live like strangers under the same roof. There's a palpable tension and discomfort between them. However, as time passes, they start to understand each other's pain and become friends. They share their pasts and find solace in each other's company. Their friendship gradually blossoms into love. However, misunderstandings and conflicts arise, leading to their separation. The climax of the movie brings a dramatic twist when Regina meets with an accident. This incident makes John realize his deep love for Regina. In the end, they reunite, resolving their misunderstandings, and decide to give their marriage a second chance. They start a new chapter of their life, filled with love and understanding. The movie beautifully portrays how love can heal the deepest wounds and give life a new meaning.

  • Release Date: 27 Sept 2013
  • Cast: Arya, Jai, Nazriya Nazim and Nayanthara
  • Director: Atlee
